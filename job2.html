Marketing Manager at MarketMasters

Location: KIGALI, Muhima

Application Deadline: June 30, 2024

Start Date: July 15, 2024

Job Description

We are seeking a talented and experienced Marketing Manager to join our team at MarketMasters. The Marketing Manager will be responsible for developing and implementing marketing strategies to promote our products and services, attract new customers, and enhance brand awareness.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop and execute comprehensive marketing plans and strategies to drive business growth.
  • Manage all aspects of marketing campaigns, including digital marketing, social media, email marketing, and advertising.
  • Create engaging content for marketing materials, including website content, blog posts, social media posts, and press releases.
  • Conduct market research and analysis to identify trends, opportunities, and competitive threats.
  • Collaborate with internal teams and external partners to develop and launch new products and campaigns.
  • Monitor and analyze marketing performance metrics to track the effectiveness of campaigns and initiatives.
  • Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices in marketing and digital technologies.
  • Manage marketing budgets and allocate resources effectively to achieve marketing goals.
  • Lead and motivate a team of marketing professionals to achieve departmental objectives.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Business Administration, or related field.
  • Proven experience as a Marketing Manager or similar role, with a track record of success in developing and executing marketing strategies.
  • Strong understanding of marketing principles, techniques, and best practices.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to build relationships and collaborate effectively with team members and stakeholders.
  • Experience in digital marketing, including SEO, SEM, social media marketing, and email marketing.
  • Ability to think creatively and strategically, with strong problem-solving skills.
  • Strong analytical skills and the ability to interpret data and metrics to inform decision-making.
  • Highly organized with excellent project management skills and attention to detail.
  • Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic environment and manage multiple projects simultaneously.
  • Experience managing and leading a team is preferred.

Application Process:

If you meet the above requirements and are passionate about marketing, we encourage you to apply for this position. Please submit your resume, cover letter, and any relevant supporting documents to uaudile@gmail.com by June 30, 2024.
