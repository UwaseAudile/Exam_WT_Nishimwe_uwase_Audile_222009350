Software Education of Mathematics in Secondary School

Location: NYAGATARE, G.S MUHURA

Application Deadline: June 30, 2024

Start Date: July 15, 2024

Job Description

We are seeking a passionate and dedicated educator to join our team as a Mathematics Teacher for Secondary School. The successful candidate will be responsible for delivering engaging and effective mathematics lessons to students, fostering a positive learning environment, and helping students develop their mathematical skills and understanding.

Responsibilities:

  • Plan and deliver high-quality mathematics lessons for secondary school students.
  • Create engaging learning materials and resources to support student learning.
  • Differentiate instruction to meet the diverse needs of students.
  • Assess student progress and provide constructive feedback.
  • Collaborate with colleagues to develop and implement curriculum initiatives.
  • Maintain accurate records of student attendance, grades, and progress.
  • Communicate regularly with parents/guardians about student progress and concerns.
  • Participate in professional development opportunities to enhance teaching skills and knowledge.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree in Mathematics Education or related field.
  • Teaching certification/license in Mathematics (Secondary Education).
  • Proven experience teaching mathematics at the secondary school level.
  • Strong knowledge of secondary school mathematics curriculum and instructional strategies.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Ability to create a supportive and inclusive learning environment.
  • Strong organizational skills and attention to detail.
  • Commitment to continuous professional growth and improvement.
  • Experience with educational technology and digital learning resources is desirable.

Application Process:

If you meet the above requirements and are passionate about educating secondary school students in mathematics, we encourage you to apply for this position. Please submit your resume, cover letter, and any relevant supporting documents to uaudile@gmail.com
