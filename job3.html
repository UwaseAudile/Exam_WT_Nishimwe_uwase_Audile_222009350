Data Scientist at DataWorld

Location: Huye, University of Rwanda

Application Deadline: July 31, 2024

Start Date: August 15, 2024

Job Description

We are looking for a highly skilled and experienced Data Scientist to join our team at DataWorld. The Data Scientist will be responsible for analyzing complex datasets, developing predictive models, and extracting valuable insights to inform business decisions and drive innovation.

Responsibilities:

  • Collect, clean, and preprocess large datasets from various sources.
  • Apply advanced statistical and machine learning techniques to analyze data and extract actionable insights.
  • Develop predictive models to forecast business trends, customer behavior, and market dynamics.
  • Design and implement algorithms for data mining, pattern recognition, and anomaly detection.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify business opportunities and solve complex problems.
  • Communicate findings and insights to stakeholders through reports, presentations, and data visualizations.
  • Stay current with the latest developments in data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence.
  • Contribute to the development of data-driven products and solutions.
  • Ensure data quality, integrity, and security throughout the data lifecycle.
  • Provide mentorship and guidance to junior members of the data science team.

Requirements:

  • Master's or Ph.D. degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or a related field.
  • Proven experience as a Data Scientist or similar role, with a strong track record of delivering impactful data-driven solutions.
  • Expertise in statistical analysis, machine learning, and data mining techniques.
  • Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, R, or Java.
  • Experience with data visualization tools and libraries (e.g., Matplotlib, Seaborn, Tableau).
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to work with large and complex datasets.
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills, with the ability to explain technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders.
  • Experience with big data technologies (e.g., Hadoop, Spark) is a plus.
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a fast-paced, multidisciplinary environment.
  • Commitment to continuous learning and professional development.

Application Process:

If you meet the above requirements and are passionate about leveraging data to drive innovation, we encourage you to apply for this position. Please submit your resume, cover letter, and any relevant supporting documents to uaudile@gmail.com by July 31, 2024.
